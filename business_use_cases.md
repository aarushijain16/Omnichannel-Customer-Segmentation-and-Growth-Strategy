# Business Use Cases

This project translates omnichannel analytics into strategic use cases relevant for lifestyle and design-led brands like Nicobar.


# 1. CRM Personalization

- Tailor communication by customer persona

- Avoid over-discounting premium segments

- Re-engage at-risk high-value customers with experiential nudges



# 2. Collection & Merchandising Strategy

- Use product affinity insights to curate intent-based collections
  (e.g., Hosting, Comfort Living, Gifting)

- Align drops with high-CLV segments



# 3. Omnichannel Experience Design

- Identify customers who browse online but convert in-store

- Optimize store layouts for high dwell-time segments

- Personalize digital UX based on channel preference



# 4. Retention Without Discounting

- Shift retention levers from price to engagement

- Use loyalty score and churn proxy to trigger soft interventions

- Focus win-back efforts on Premium but At-Risk cluster



# 5. Strategic Growth Decisions

- Identify clusters driving revenue vs long-term value

- Balance short-term sales with brand equity

- Support leadership decisions with behavioral evidence
